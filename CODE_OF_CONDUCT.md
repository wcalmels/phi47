# Code of Conduct

## Purpose

Phi47 is an independent scientific software and research project. Participation
must support rigorous, respectful, and reproducible technical discussion.

## Expected conduct

Contributors and participants are expected to:

- discuss evidence, methods, code, and results rather than personal attributes;
- distinguish verified results from hypotheses, conjectures, and interpretation;
- provide reproducible examples when reporting technical problems;
- acknowledge uncertainty and limitations;
- cite relevant prior work when making scientific claims;
- accept constructive technical criticism;
- protect private or confidential information.

## Unacceptable conduct

The following behavior is not accepted:

- harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks;
- deliberate misrepresentation of results or credentials;
- fabricated benchmarks, citations, evidence, or test results;
- disclosure of private information without authorization;
- disruption of technical discussions;
- plagiarism or removal of legitimate attribution;
- presenting speculative claims as experimentally established facts.

## Scientific disagreements

Disagreement is not misconduct. Competing interpretations are welcome when they
are expressed professionally and supported by mathematics, code, experiments,
references, or clearly identified reasoning.

## Enforcement

Potential violations may be reported privately to:

**wcalmels@phi47.cl**

The repository owner may edit, hide, or remove content and may restrict
participation when necessary to protect the project and its contributors.
